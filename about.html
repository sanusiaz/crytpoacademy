About Me

How I got here ?

From the early days of my career in finance, I’ve always been driven by a desire to understand and master the complexities of the financial markets. I started out as a stock market strategist, but it didn’t take long for me to see the transformative potential of cryptocurrencies and decide to shift my focus.
Over the past 7 years, I’ve immersed myself in the world of crypto trading. My background in stocks has given me a unique perspective, allowing me to merge traditional market principles with the ever-evolving landscape of cryptocurrencies. This blend of expertise has made me a go-to voice in the industry.

3 THINGS I PROVIDE

Throughout my journey, I’ve had the privilege of advising a range of clients, from startups and established crypto exchanges to individual investors. I’ve helped them navigate the often tumultuous waters of the crypto market with a steady hand and clear strategy. My insights have been featured in major financial publications, and I’m often invited to share my knowledge at international crypto conferences.
Beyond just trading, I’m passionate about education. I’ve developed and taught courses on everything from crypto trading strategies to risk management and market analysis. When I’m not trading or teaching, you’ll often find me diving deep into blockchain research, always eager to stay ahead of the curve and ensure my clients and students benefit from the latest market insights.
